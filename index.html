About Sophie

From working 9-5, to full time online; I have been able to build a successful online marketing business that has provided me with complete freedom.

From a young age I knew that I didn't want to spend the next 45+ years working for someone else! I spent 4 years in a normal 9-5 job, and to be honest, I hated it! I was desperate for a way out.

A year ago I came across an opportunity that allowed me to start an online business. I never realised how much potential the online world had until I got started. I can now work when and wherever I choose and the potential to earn is unlimited!

I'm now offering this opportunity to others who are ready for change and who want to stop trading their precious time for money!
